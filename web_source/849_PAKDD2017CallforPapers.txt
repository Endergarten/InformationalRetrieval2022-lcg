								  <h1 class="arti_title">PAKDD 2017 Call for Papers</h1>
				  <p class="arti_metas"><span class="arti_publisher">发布者：</span><span class="arti_update">发布时间：2016-10-11</span><span class="arti_views">浏览次数：<span class="WP_VisitCount" url="/_visitcountdisplay?siteId=289&type=3&articleId=255486">97</span></span></p>
				  <div class="entry">
					<div class="read"><div class='wp_articlecontent'><h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 1.6em; font-family: 'Open Sans', Arial, sans-serif; font-variant: normal; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-transform: none; color: blue; font-style: normal; widows: 1; margin: 0.2em 0px; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-indent: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"> Call for Papers</h2><div style="box-sizing: border-box; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-transform: none; color: rgb(0,0,0); font: 16px 'Open Sans', Arial, sans-serif; widows: 1; margin: 0px; letter-spacing: normal; text-indent: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"> <div class="margin-bottom" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 1.25em">  <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 1.2em; color: black; margin: 0.2em 0px">   Important Dates</h3>  <ul style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Paper submission due: 11:59:59 p.m. PST, October 30 (Sun), 2016</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Notification to authors: January 13 (Fri), 2017</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Camera-ready due: February 13 (Mon), 2017</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Conference: May 23 (Tues) - 26 (Fri), 2017</li>  </ul> </div> <div class="margin-bottom" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 1.25em">  <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 1.2em; color: black; margin: 0.2em 0px">   Conference Scope</h3>  <p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; text-indent: 10px">   The Pacific-Asia Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (PAKDD) is a leading international conference in the areas of knowledge discovery and data mining (KDD). It provides an international forum for researchers and industry practitioners to share their new ideas, original research results, and practical development experiences from all KDD related areas, including data mining, data warehousing, machine learning, artificial intelligence, databases, statistics, knowledge engineering, visualization, decision-making systems, and the emerging applications.</p> </div> <div class="margin-bottom" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 1.25em">  <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 1.2em; color: black; margin: 0.2em 0px">   Topics</h3>  <p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; text-indent: 10px">   The topics of relevance for the conference papers include but not limited to the following:</p>  <ul style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Theoretic foundations</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Novel models and algorithms</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Association analysis</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Clustering</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Classification</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Statistical methods for data mining</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Data pre-processing</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Feature extraction and selection</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Post-processing including quality assessment and validation</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining heterogeneous/multi-source data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining sequential data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining spatial and temporal data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining unstructured and semi-structured data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining graph and network data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining social networks</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining high dimensional data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining uncertain data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining imbalanced data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining dynamic/streaming data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining behavioral data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining multimedia data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Mining scientific data</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Privacy preserving data mining</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Anomaly detection</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Fraud and risk analysis</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Security and intrusion detection</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Visual data mining</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Interactive and online mining</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Ubiquitous knowledge discovery and agent-based data mining</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Integration of data warehousing, OLAP, and data mining</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Parallel, distributed, and cloud-based high performance data mining</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Opinion mining and sentiment analysis</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Human, domain, organizational, and social factors in data mining</li>   <li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">    Applications to healthcare, bioinformatics, computational chemistry, finance, eco-informatics, marketing, gaming, cyber-security etc.</li>  </ul> </div> <div class="margin-bottom" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 1.25em">  <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 1.2em; color: black; margin: 0.2em 0px">   Paper Submission</h3>  <p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; text-indent: 10px">   The submitted paper should adhere to the<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px">double-blind</strong><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span>review policy. All papers will be double-blind reviewed by the Program Committee on the basis of technical quality, relevance to data mining, originality, significance, and clarity. All paper submissions will be handled electronically. Detailed instructions are provided on the conference home page.
